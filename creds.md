Indian official documents mapped to extractable verifiable credentials for an SSI system:

Aadhaar Card → Age/DOB, Address, Gender, Identity (Name + UID), Residency
PAN Card → Financial Identity (PAN), Name Verification, Taxpayer Status
Passport → Citizenship, Age/DOB, Identity (High Trust), Address, Travel Eligibility
Driving License → Age Verification (18+/21+), Driving Eligibility (Vehicle Classes), Address, Identity
Voter ID → Citizenship (Voter Status), Age ≥ 18, Address, Identity
Birth Certificate → Date of Birth, Place of Birth, Legal Identity Proof
Ration Card → Family Membership, Economic Status (BPL/APL), Address, Scheme Eligibility
Income Certificate → Income Bracket, Economic Eligibility (EWS/BPL)
Caste Certificate → Caste Category (SC/ST/OBC/General), Reservation Eligibility
Domicile Certificate → State Residency, Local Eligibility
Education Certificates → Qualification Level, Institution Verification, Marks/Grades
Employee/Work Certificate → Employment Status, Organization, Role, Work Experience
